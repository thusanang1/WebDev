FILING OF ANNUAL RETURNS AND BUSINESS NAME RENEWALS
Pursuant to the Companies Act and the Registration of Business Names Act, all companies must file an annual return every year, and all business names must file a renewal every three years
To file an annual return or business name renewal, a company secretary, company director or business name proprietor must have authority over the entity on the online system. Please refer to the information centre section of this website for instructions on how to get authority.
